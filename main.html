The Train
and The City

São Paulo is the largest city in Latin America, it is a city of sound, people, culture, and also a city of transportation. In the urban daily life of São Paulo public transportation plays a vital role. With extensive lines of bus, light rail and subway the whole network constantly seeks ways to keep the city moving. The intent of this map is to provide a glimpse into this world. By focusing on the subway and light rail infrastructure you will explore how such networks interacts with the city. Ridership numbers are provided so you can have an idea of how crucial these lines are for São Paulo.
